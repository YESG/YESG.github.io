Project HOPE

Provide Health, Opportunity, Provisions, and Education to those in need in Gujurat, India.

Healthcare

YES-Good contributes to funding that has provided diverse surgeries and medical equipment to the needy in Bhavnagar, Gujurat for over 10 years. Life changing surgeriesThese can include wheelchairs, crutches, hearing aids, cataract surgeries, artificial legs for polio victims, and rubella vaccinations.

Opportunity

We believe that everybody should have the opportunity to earn a livelihood and be a productive member of society. We are kick starting a small craft store for graduates of a cerebral palsy and severe autism vocational school in India and have connected them with customers across the globe. We want to provide the opportunity for everybody to live meaningful lives.

Provisions

There is a large portion of disabled adults with no support system. They have lost their ability to work and have a difficulty earning a living. Thus, we provide monthly support to a group of families in the form of 5 kilograms of assorted dals, grain, rice, and other foodstuffs off of which they can survive.

Education

Education is an integral part of modern society and thus we support public and free education for everybody. Therefore we have been supporting the K.L Institute for the Deaf which currently teaches K-12 students. We are also assisting them in their plans to expand to the university level and are serving as liasons between the K.L Institute for the Deaf (an Indian school that currently only offers K-12 education) and Gallaudet University (which provides undergraduate and graduate education degrees in America).
